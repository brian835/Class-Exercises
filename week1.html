In Kilimani, Nairobi, the web developer salary varies significantly based on experience and seniority. Entry-level web developers can expect to earn between KES 20,000 and KES 50,000 monthly, while mid-level developers with 2-5 years of experience can earn KES 60,000 to KES 120,000 monthly. Senior web developers with over 5 years of experience can earn KES 150,000 to KES 300,000 or more per month.

So if you are looking to start a career in web development, Kilimani offers a vibrant tech community and numerous opportunities for growth.
